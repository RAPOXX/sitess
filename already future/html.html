Future Of Work

The number of robots in the world today
is approaching 1,000,000, with almost half that
number in Japan and just 15% in the US. A
couple of decades ago, 90% of robots were
used in car manufacturing, typically on
assembly lines doing a variety of repetitive
tasks. Today only 50% are in automobile
plants, with the other half spread out
among other factories, and many other
industries.
