ABOUT THE EDUCATION FOUNDATION

The Manned Space Flight Education Foundation inonprofit educational foundation offering extensive science education
programs and a space museum. The cornerstone of its education mission is Space Center Houston, a leading science and space
exploration learning center. It is one of Houston’s top attractions, the area’s No. 1 attraction for international visitors, the
Official Visitor Center of NASA Johnson Space Center and a Smithsonian Affiliate.
